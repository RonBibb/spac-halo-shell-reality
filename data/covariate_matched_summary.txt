INTERPRETATION
==============================================================================

  Full sample T_z: β = -0.043, p = 0.927

  T-type DOES NOT SURVIVE in any matched-quality subsample.
  Interpretation: the confounding between T-type and observational
  quality is structural in SPARC. The galaxy-level morphology gradient
  cannot be cleanly separated from data-quality covariates. §3.1's
  framing should be revised: report the T-correlated shell-bearing rate
  as a sample-level regularity that is statistically inseparable from
  observational covariates, not as a clean morphological effect.

  Note: per-shell findings (M-r, σ-r, inner-vs-outer, σ/r) are unaffected.
  This result concerns galaxy-level shell-bearing classification only.
